How It Works

Simple 4-step process to get food from donor to the needy

1
🏪

Donor Lists Food

Restaurants, bakeries, and events post surplus food with expiry time and pickup address.

2
🏢

NGO Claims It

Verified NGOs browse nearby listings and claim food before it expires.

3
🚴

Volunteer Picks Up

Assigned volunteers collect food from the donor and deliver to the NGO.

4
🌍

Impact Tracked

Every delivery records kg saved, meals provided, and CO₂ prevented.
